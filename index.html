01 About

I’m Brooks, a software developer. When I'm not at work, I love creating cool projects like this website, practicing photography, playing guitar, reading books and manga, and watching films and anime.

I decided to create this website as a way to track all those hobbies in one place that's easy to access and so other people can view what I'm up to. There's also a journal section where I write about my thoughts and experiences.

Combining APIs from Goodreads, MyAnimeList, Letterboxd, Spotify, and osu!, I'm able to give live updates on what media I'm consuming.

If you like this site and its design, feel free to check out my work portfolio on the right. It showcases some of the other projects I've worked on outside of work, and gives more insight into my professional experience and skills.

If you have any questions or simply want to say hi, you can reach me via email (which is also on the right sidebar).
